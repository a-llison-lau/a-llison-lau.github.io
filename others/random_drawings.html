Random Drawings

Welcome to my illustrations portfolio! These illustrations were created with pencil and paper in a simple line style. My inspiration for starting these illustrations came from the gameOmori, a surreal RPG game that follows the story of a young boy named Omori and his adventures in the dream world. The game is known for its unique and captivating storyline, engaging gameplay mechanics, and stunning graphics. The artwork in the game features a distinct style characterized by simple lines, pastel colors, and cute, chibi-like characters.

Inspired by the game's style, I began to create my own illustrations using pencil and paper last year (I did not have an iPad back then). I am drawn to the simplicity and whimsy of Omori's character designs. I also realized that I could use my illustrations to capture the moments and memories of my friends in a unique and personal way (before things went shit oh no 💩). So, I started drawing my friends in the style of Omori, creating simple depictions of our shared experiences. I think drawing them not only trained my drawing skills or creativity, it also made me become more observant, such as paying attention the clothes they wear, the brands they like, their shoes, accessories, stories behind those accessories, and from there, I have a better understanding of their personalities and styles. It's a good training really.
